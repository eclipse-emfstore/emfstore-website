Maintenance mode

EMFStore was declared end-of-life (EOL) by December 31st 2022. There will be no more releases unless they are required by our customers. In case you require updates please approach us, we offer LTS (Long-term-support) packages to allow you to continue using EMFStore after EOL.

If you start a new project and consider to use EMFStore please consider other options like EMF Compare and CDO. We have extensive knowledge in building solutions for model versioning and also offer commercial consulting and support on choosing a framework for this purpose. Get in contact with us to discuss details.
